How It Works

From listing to closed deal — a streamlined process designed for speed and trust.

1

Seller Lists Property

Submit your abandoned or distressed property. Address stays blurred until a buyer is approved.

2

Admin Reviews

Our team verifies the listing and approves it for the marketplace — keeping quality high.

3

Buyer Expresses Interest

Vetted buyers browse listings and express interest. One click starts the deal flow.

4

Deal Protected & Closed

NCA auto-generates. Full address unlocked. Deal moves to closing.
